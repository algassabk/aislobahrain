ABOUT THE PROJECT

What is Aislo Bahrain?

Aislo Bahrain is a smart supermarket shopping application designed to improve the in-store shopping experience in Bahrain. It helps customers find products, manage their budget, organize shopping lists, view offers, and plan meals.

THE PROBLEM

Why Aislo?

🧭 Product Location

Customers often spend extra time searching for products inside large supermarkets.

💸 Budget Control

Many shoppers exceed their planned budget because they cannot track spending while shopping.

📝 Forgotten Items

Customers may forget important products when they shop without an organized list.

OUR SOLUTION

Shopping Made Smarter

Aislo Bahrain combines smart shopping tools in one application. The app helps users search for products, view offers, manage their cart, track their budget, prepare meals, and navigate supermarket aisles easily. It also supports managers by providing useful dashboards and reports.

HOW IT WORKS

Simple Shopping Journey

Aislo guides users through a smart and simple shopping experience, from start to finish.

1

Select Store

The user chooses the supermarket before starting the shopping journey.

2

Explore Products

The user searches products, categories, offers, and meal ingredients.

3

Use Smart Tools

The user uses smart tools like budget planner, shopping lists, meal prep, and aisle navigation.

4

Add To Cart

The user adds selected products and reviews the shopping cart.

5

Checkout

The user completes the order with a clearer and more organized shopping process.
